# Kura: Unified Health Management

## Brand Name
**Kura**

*   **Pronunciation:** /ˈkʉːrɑ/ (similar to koo-rah)
*   **Meaning:** Invented, easy to pronounce, and not found in dictionaries.

## Concept Overview
Kura is a comprehensive web application designed to streamline health management for two primary user groups: adult children coordinating care for aging or ill parents, and patients managing complex or chronic conditions.

## Target Audience
1.  **Adult children with aging or ill parents**
2.  **Patients with complex or chronic conditions**

## Solution: Kura - Unified Health Management

### 1. Caregiver Coordination Module
- Shared Calendar, Medical Log, Task Management, Document Storage

### 2. Medical Timeline Module
- Personal Health Log, Visual Timeline, Doctor-Friendly Summary
